The article examines how research translates into policy decisions using China as a case study, highlighting China's structured five-step policymaking process where research plays a foundational role. It discusses the importance of open access research for policy impact and provides practical recommendations for researchers seeking to increase their work's real-world influence on sustainable development goals.
