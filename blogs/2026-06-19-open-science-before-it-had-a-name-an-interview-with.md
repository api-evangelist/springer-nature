An interview with Dr. Steven Henikoff, a molecular biologist at Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Centre, on his decades-long advocacy for openly sharing scientific methods, data, and protocols well before the term "open science" became common. Henikoff frames the motivation across his 50+ year career: methods are only successful if someone else uses them.
